Project Details

For this Case Study, I chose two task flows to analyze on the Uber Eats app. The first flow is the main flow where a comsumer is ordering food and the second flow involves a consumer seeking help. I used Jakob Nielsen's 10 general principles for interaction design to determine the heuristics issues, and gave them a severity rating based on Nielson's severity ratings for usability problems.
